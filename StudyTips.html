Study Tips

Ways to reduce academic pressure

Planning

Be organized. Use a planner or calendar to break big tasks into smaller, manageable steps.

Breaks

Take regular breaks to avoid burnout. Try the Pomodoro method — 25 minutes on, 5 minutes off.

Focus

Avoid distractions. Put your phone away and find a quiet space to study effectively.
